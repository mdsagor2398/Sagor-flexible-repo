National Parliament Election 2024

Millions of Bangladeshi citizens voted in the National Parliament Election 2024 on January 7. Voting took place from 8 am to 4 pmAmong those who have been invited as full ministers in the new cabinet, the current cabinet's commerce minister Tipu Munshi, foreign minister Abdul Momen, finance minister AHM Mustafa Kamal, agriculture minister Dr. Abdur Razzaq, Health Minister Zahid Malek and Planning Minister MA Mannan..
